Obesity

Obesity is a chronic, complex and multifactorial disease that usually begins in childhood and adolescence, that has its origin in a genetic and environmental interaction, is the most prevalent metabolic disease in the western world being a source of great morbidity and mortality.

So when we talk about obesity We do it as a serious thing.

The purpose of the project is to show how obesity is distributed in different regions of a country and how it affects people and with that aware institutions to create healthcare programs.
